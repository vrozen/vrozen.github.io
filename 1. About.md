My name is Riemer van Rozen.

As a Software Language Engineer, I have over fifteen years of experience in programming language development, consultancy, and R&D. Together with domain experts, I develop languages, techniques, and tools that improve software, accelerate its design and simplify its maintenance. By formalizing domain knowledge, business logic and rules I translate complex business challenges into practical solutions. This results in reliable systems for automated workflows, verifiable reasoning, and informed decision-making.

My favorite approach is *"Build it to understand it"*.
I create programming languages, tools and techniques that are:
1) *domain-specific* solutions for non-programmer experts and novices;
and 2) *language-parametric* technology for 
analyzing software and developing domain-specific languages.

Here, on my personal home page and [blog](/Blog/), 
I share updates about my activities and output.
