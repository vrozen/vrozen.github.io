Welcome!

This website is an interactive version of a systematic mapping study on
"Languages of Games and Play" [1] based on Chapter 2 of my PhD thesis [2].
It wraps the journal article (and its appendix) and provides additional features.
  
*  The main view shows a graph of publications (nodes)
   and citations (edges) between them.
*  Click on the nodes to inspect publication info.
*  Zoom in and out for inspecting parts of the graph.   
*  Rearch and filter by author name, publication title or year
   or select a language by using the controls on the left.
*  Toggle the PDF viewer on and off for reading the paper,
   and finding summaries of the languages.
*  To close the welcome message click the button below.

I hope this map can assist readers in identifying relevant publications,
navigating between research areas and technological spaces,
and charting new trajectories for research and development.
The website is intended as a 'living document' that
through maintenance and extensions remains up-to-date.
In case you want to cite the article, please find the BibTeX below.

Please let me know if you have comments, questions or if you found bugs.
I would be happy to discuss my findings!
